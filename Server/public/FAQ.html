FAQ

  • What is this?

    It's a website where you can control the LED strip lights in my room.

  • Why?

    I like LEDs, and I wanted to be able to control them from my phone.

  • Technical Details?

    I have 40 feet of WS2801B led strips mounted to the top border of my room, powered by an old PC power supply and controlled by an Arduino UNO. A RasPi hosts this web server, and uses Node to communicate with clients (like you!) and to send color updates to the Arduino over serial/USB.
